# KennelFlow Boarding 0.2.6

Since WordPress.org **0.1.2**.

## Staff calendar & front desk

- Front-end `[kennelflow_boarding_calendar]` and boarding-default Hub calendar for desk staff.
- Boarding desk capabilities (`edit_kennelflow_boarding_bookings`, Hub pets); Confirm / Check in workflows.
- Stable `kf_bookings` index row IDs on status change (Check in).
- Clinic appointment fields: reason for visit and appointment notes on hub bookings.
- Clinical staff can open clinic rows from the shared calendar.

## Stay photos

- **Stay photos** on boarding stays: check-in and check-out slots in the staff calendar popover and admin booking editor.
- Take photo (device camera) and Choose photo; REST API and session media table.
- Desk roles granted `upload_files` for calendar uploads (0.2.6).

See `readme.txt` for 0.1.3–0.2.6 details.
